# Hi, I'm Nick Costello

This is not your traditional "personal website" by any means. At the core, this is a _blog_, but really, these are just my notes
in public. I'm not going to try and sell you anything, convince you to hire me,
or use the tools I like. I'm a software engineer. **I enjoy learning about technology and I think there's a benefit to writing about it.**
Here's how this is all laid out:

- On the left, you'll find a post or a collection of posts on a topic I've written.
- In the middle, you'll find the post itself.
- On the right, you'll find a functional table of contents above a button to provide me feedback.

Hopefully, you'll find something useful here. If not, well, it's free to read so I guess you got what you paid for.
